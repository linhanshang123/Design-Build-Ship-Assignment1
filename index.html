Assignment 1 Archive

Personal Landing Page Iterations

A simple index of landing page versions exploring different directions for a personal site.

Phase 1

Wide Exploration

v01 Pixel Lobby

A clean personal landing page shaped by retro game panels, pixel borders, and a calm arcade mood.

 v02 Feature Issue

An editorial landing page that frames standout achievements as magazine cover lines, spreads, and spotlight stories.

 v03 Sunset Signal

A vaporwave-led landing page mixing bubble-era twilight, coastal highways, cold post-punk dusk, and rock-driven intensity.

 v04 Bianjing Scroll

A Chinese-inspired landing page blending Henan roots, everyday warmth, classical romance, and contemporary city life.

 v05 Signal Fracture

An avant-garde landing page that collides music, competition, research, culture, and personal memory in a disruptive composition.

 v06 Competitive Index

A Swiss-inspired personal dossier that arranges gaming, research, singing, and academic trajectory inside a crisp institutional grid.

 v07 Moonlit Garden

A fairy-tale personal landing page with floral wallpaper, storybook cards, and a soft theatrical structure for games, research, music, and study.

 v08 Underworld Ascent

A mythic underworld landing page with volcanic color, ceremonial frames, and a combat-HUD structure inspired by roguelike intensity.

 v09 Neon Gridline

A cyberpunk command-board with a giant display stage, stacked neon modules, and a future-city route map for gaming, research, and performance.

 v10 Astral Save Point

A dreamlike JRPG title-screen page with command menus, save-slot cards, a world map route, and a fantasy dialogue box built around your personal story.

 v11 Phantom Route

A red-black-white phantom-heist layout built from jagged dialogue boxes, slashed menu cards, and irregular combat-style panels.

 v12 Pink House Reverie

A romantic pink personal site shaped like a sweet little home, with cozy rooms, soft light, and affectionate domestic atmosphere.

 v13 Neon Atlas

A fullscreen cyberpunk mosaic that arranges identity as a tablet-like grid of neon tiles, mixing games, research, voice, and future-city atmosphere.

Phase 2

Narrowing And Combination

v14 Neon Atlas Route

A Phase 2 hybrid that keeps the fullscreen cyberpunk mosaic from v13, adds the stronger route map logic from v09, and sharpens the title typography with v02's editorial serif voice.

 v15 Neon Afterglow

A softer hybrid of the neon atlas layout and vaporwave gradients, bringing concise cyberpunk framing together with your city-pop, post-punk, and rock listening profile.

 v16 Ember Save Point

A hybrid that keeps v10's JRPG title-screen structure and typography, then pushes it through v08's underworld palette, combat-panel edges, and Hades-like intensity.

 v17 Moon Court Garden

A Chinese courtyard hybrid that keeps v07's storybook garden flow, then rebuilds it with v04's pavilion atmosphere, night-court palette, and room-like grid inspired by traditional courtyards.

 v18 Sakura Garden Reverie

A garden-travel hybrid that keeps v07's storybook structure while borrowing v12's pink palette, softer warmth, and small romantic details, with sakura accents replacing the background dots.

 v19 Shanglin Chronicle

A moonlit-garden JRPG hybrid that keeps v10's title-screen structure and hero title, then turns the journey into a romantic night adventure of clearing chapter after chapter beneath petals and silver paths.

Phase 3

Refinement Toward Final Direction

v21 Shanglin Chronicle: Five Chapters

A direct refinement of v19 that keeps the moonlit JRPG chronicle, then expands it into five long-form chapters on one scrolling page, each with its own hero-title moment and evolving color atmosphere.
