Opening Sequence

One more chapter beneath the moonlit garden.

This version keeps the dreamlike JRPG frame from v10, but the world has changed. The journey now unfolds inside a moonlit garden, where each chapter feels like another night stage to clear, another quiet trial to survive, and another place where growth happens without losing romance.

I want the page to feel like entering a night instance full of silver paths, flower light, and the promise that every cleared chapter changes the traveler who returns from it.
