A personal website as a sweet little home

Shanglin, arranged in warm light and soft pink rooms.

This version treats my personal website like a small, affectionate home: gentle, luminous, and quietly full of the things that matter. Not loud ambition, but a lived-in atmosphere where games, research, music, and memory all feel close to the heart.

Living Room

I still love games deeply, especially worlds worth returning to.

Even in a softer version of my site, gaming remains central. I love systems that reward patience, rhythm, and courage, especially roguelikes that make every attempt feel intimate, difficult, and strangely personal.

Study Corner

Research is the quiet room where curiosity stays awake.

My work in recommender systems is about tracing preference, discovery, and behavior with care. I like questions that make hidden structure visible without losing the human texture inside them.

Music Shelf

Singing keeps the whole home from becoming too silent.

Voice adds tenderness to discipline. It gives room for breath, phrasing, and emotional color, which matters because I never want precision to become cold.

Little Letter

I want at least one version of my website to feel like love made architectural.

Not in the narrow sense of romance alone, but in the broader sense of care: choosing a warm color, softening an edge, keeping a lamp on, and arranging every detail so the space says someone thoughtful lives here.
