v07 / Fairy Tale Exploration

A softer mythology for a competitive life

Shanglin in the moonlit garden.

This version imagines a personal website as an enchanted pavilion: part conservatory, part diary, part stage, where ranked intensity, recommender systems research, singing, and academic travel can all belong to the same gentle world.

moon birdsong petals

First Chapter

The player beneath the lanterns.

In one world I play League of Legends with a serious competitive instinct. I like the hidden geometry of the map, the rhythm of pressure, and the exact second where patience becomes action.

Even in a fairy-tale frame, that instinct stays sharp: reading movement, predicting intention, and turning uncertainty into an advantage before the field fully reveals itself.

Second Chapter

The scholar of recommendation trails.

My research interest is recommender systems, where patterns behave like footprints in a forest. I am drawn to how models learn preference, how discovery can feel surprising rather than mechanical, and how intelligence should guide rather than flatten.

Third Chapter

The singer in the glasshouse.

Singing is the part of me that insists knowledge should also breathe. Voice work teaches control, phrasing, and presence, but it also keeps emotion near the surface instead of hiding everything inside pure analysis.
