v03 / Vaporwave Exploration

Personal Landing Page / Music Memory Interface

Between sunset neon, frozen dusk, and noon-bright noise.

A vaporwave-first version of a personal site for someone who hears whole landscapes inside music: coastal highways at golden hour, cold post-punk air over open ground, and the violent clarity of rock in full daylight.

Transmission

The site is not trying to imitate an album cover. It is trying to turn listening into atmosphere, memory, and identity.

City Pop / Vaporwave

Bubble-era twilight on a highway by the sea.

Think reflective glass, fading pink light, dashboard glow, and the calm illusion of endless motion. This is the part of the profile drawn toward Mariya Takeuchi, Miki Matsubara, and the dream of a city still shining after the sun has already started to leave.

Post-Punk

Cold evening air and the dignity of sadness.

In another register, the mood becomes distant and northern. The palette cools, the rhythm tightens, and the emotional center moves toward the stark ache carried by bands like Motorama and Corn Wave.

Rock

White daylight, friction, and a living pulse.

Then the page opens into heat and force: the feeling of wanting more from life, pushing back against numbness, and finding urgency in bands like No Party For Cao Dong, Deca Joins, and Escape Plan.

Three Signals

One identity, tuned by different frequencies.

18:42

Sunset Drive

Palm silhouettes, soft chrome, pink-to-apricot sky, and the sensation that a song can stretch one minute of dusk into an entire emotional architecture.

20:17

Cold Territory

The scene shifts into steel blue, black water, and a quieter form of intensity where distance, grief, and restraint become their own kind of beauty.

12:08

Open Daylight

Everything sharpens. The emotional field is no longer reflective but explosive, restless, and fully awake.

Profile

I want a personal site that feels like a moodboard with a pulse.

The visual direction is vaporwave, but the personality inside it is broader than nostalgia. It is about emotional range, strong taste, and the ability to move between elegance, melancholy, and force without losing coherence.
