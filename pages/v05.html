Personal landing page as collision

I want the website to feel like impact, memory, and taste happening at the same time.

I am shaped by competitive games, post-punk cold, city-pop twilight, rock intensity, Chinese cultural memory, Henan food, research discipline, and the need for a visual language that does not flatten any of it.

Working premise

Identity is stronger when it resists neat packaging.

Position

I do not want a site that behaves like a static resume. I want one that behaves like a field of signals, where biography, aesthetics, and ambition overlap.

Research instinct

Systems matter to me. Patterns matter to me. Precision matters to me. That applies as much to code and recommendation research as it does to games, rhythm, and visual composition.

I want elegance without politeness. Emotion without blur. Culture without costume. Noise without losing clarity.

One line

This is what happens when a gamer, music obsessive, researcher, and culturally rooted person refuses to become one clean category.
