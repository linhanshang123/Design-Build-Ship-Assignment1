v08 / Mythic Underworld Exploration

Shanglin / Gamer / Researcher / Singer / Endless Run

Fight upward. Learn deeply. Return stronger.

After carrying so many identities at once, I still come back to games, especially roguelikes. They reward repetition without emptiness, skill without certainty, and style without losing discipline. That rhythm feels close to how I want my personal website to behave.

Repeated attempts are not failure. They are form.

Weapon Aspect

League of Legends trained my instinct for tempo and commitment.

Competitive play taught me to read pressure, detect windows, and commit with intent. I like games where information is partial, stakes are live, and one decision can redraw the entire field.

Boon Path

Recommender systems research is another form of pattern combat.

I study how systems infer preference, shape discovery, and respond to incomplete signals. Good recommendation design is not only about prediction. It is about making complexity usable without making people smaller than they are.

Invocation

Singing keeps the work alive.

Voice is where control becomes expression. It sharpens timing, breath, emotional range, and stage presence, which matters to me because even technical lives need some fire in them.

Journey Record

From Miami heat to Chicago rigor.
