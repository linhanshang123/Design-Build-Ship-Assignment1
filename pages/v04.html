v04 / Chinese Cultural Exploration

Zhengzhou roots / Henan memory / modern China

A personal site shaped by Bianjing streets, hot soup mornings, and city lights after dark.

I was born in Zhengzhou, Henan. I love the place that raised me: its food, its direct warmth, its history, its ordinary closeness, and the way old culture and fast modern life can exist on the same street without contradiction.

Opening Note

This version is not a generic ancient-style page. It is a riverside scroll translated into a personal landing page: part memory, part city study, part cultural self-portrait.

Henan table

Hui mian, hu la tang, and fen jiang noodles are part of my biography.

These dishes are not just local references. They are how home tastes: dense, hot, practical, generous, and impossible to separate from real daily life.

Classical feeling

I love the romance of poets, scholars, moonlight, distance, and restraint.

Classical Chinese writing teaches a different scale of emotion. A river, a tower, a departure, a late-night lamp, or a line of verse can hold an entire inner world.

Living present

I also love the speed, technology, convenience, and vivid night life of today.

The China I love is not frozen in the past. It is bright, efficient, social, and constantly moving, while still keeping a texture of human nearness.

Bianjing logic

I want my website to feel like a long walk through one living cultural landscape.

Where I come from

Henan gives me a sense of gravity. It carries historical depth, but it also feels grounded and unpretentious. That balance matters to me.

What I value

I am drawn to culture that can hold both elegance and usefulness: poetry and transit systems, handwritten feeling and digital convenience, old memory and present momentum.

How this page should feel

Calm from a distance, rich up close, and full of small details like a scroll painting where markets, bridges, homes, and sky all belong to the same world.

Intent

Not costume, not nostalgia, not museum glass.

This page aims for a Chinese-inspired identity that still feels current. Ink, paper, vermilion, rivers, rooftops, and evening glow are here, but they support a personal story about home, taste, and modern life.
